 ## F2 — Incident History

 Users view all previously submitted incidents in a list with a short content
 preview. Selecting an incident loads its full details from the database.

 ### Test Case 1 — Single Incident in List

 **Input:** Database contains one incident submitted via F1.

 **Action:** Open the app. The "Incident History" section appears below the
 submit form.

 **Expected Result:**
 - The selectbox contains one entry in the format `#1 | timestamp | preview...`
 - The preview shows the first 100 characters of the incident content.

 ### Test Case 2 — Select and View Details

 **Input:** Database contains 4 incidents.

 **Action:** Choose the third incident from the selectbox dropdown.

 **Expected Result:**
 - The full content of the correct incident is displayed below the selectbox.
 - The incident ID and timestamp are shown as a caption.
 - The content matches what was originally submitted for that incident.

 ### Test Case 3 — Empty Database

 **Input:** No incidents in the database (delete `incidents.db` and restart).

 **Action:** Open the app.

 **Expected Result:**
 - The history section shows "No incidents found."
 - The submit form (F1) continues to work and new submissions appear in
   the history after the page rerenders.
